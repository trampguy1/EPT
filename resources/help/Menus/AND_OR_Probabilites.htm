And/Or Probability Set

This set is based on the concept of and/or logic where each causal or inhibiting mechanism will default to 0 probability alone but will be grouped together with a probability of 1 for an AND Event and similar OR logic for an OR Event.  At the creation of new Events you will be prompted to choose either AND or OR defaults for that Event which will determine its behavior.  Essentially this method of modeling will result in a model with no uncertainty.  The modeler may then decide to fine tune the model adding uncertainty as required.

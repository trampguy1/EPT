Starting the Sampler

 

When you have finished constructing your Model, the Sampler is needed to determine the probability of the different Events.  The Sampler creates many instances of the world modeled by the Model.  

 

Events are randomly set to either have occurred or not occurred based on their Scheduling, Evidence, and any signals they receive or send.  A running tally is kept of the proportion of cases in which each Event occurs.  When a sufficiently large number of samples is generated, the probability of an Event can be given as the proportion of samples in which the Event had occurred.

 

To start the Sampler, simply click on the Start Sampler button   in the Model Toolbar.  JCAT will then begin generating samples, and you can monitor the number of samples created by looking at the Status Bar at the bottom of the Application Window.

Adding Threat Resources

 

Currently Threat Resources must be on the trigger nodes, that is, the nodes that are 'Scheduled'.  Remember, in addition to providing the resources, the model builder must schedule the times at which the threat events are supposed to occur.

 

To add a Threat Resource:

    1.  Point to an Event or a Mechanism.

    2.  Then right click and select Threat Resource.

    3.  Enter in desired time and allocated resources.

 

 

Note: Threat Resources can be added at different times throughout the model building process.

Scheduling

 

Scheduling sets both the time and probability of an event.  The Scheduling table displays when the Event/Mechanism is Scheduled to occur.  To Schedule the Event/Mechanism for a new timestep, enter the timestep number in the “Time” text field, and enter in the probability that the Event/Mechanism occurs at that time in the “Probability” text field.  Then click “Schedule” to add the timestep to the Scheduling table.

 

To remove a timestep from the Scheduling table, click on the row in the table for the desired timestep, and click “Delete.”  If you wish to remove all Scheduling for the Event/Mechanism, click “Delete All.

 

Tips:

 

  • Scheduling and Evidence perform similar functions.

  • Scheduling an Event/Mechanism for a block of time can be accomplished in one of two ways.

  • Evidence and Scheduling can be use for other purposes.

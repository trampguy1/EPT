Defining a Timestep

 

A timestep is an unspecified, arbitrary length of time.  Depending how you wish to view your Model, a timestep could be days, weeks, hours, or minutes.  For instance, if you are modeling an overall campaign, Events occur over the course of days, weeks or even months.  However, if you are modeling a particular mission, significant Events occur with hours or minutes.  Therefore, the creator of the Model must determine the period of time a timestep represents.

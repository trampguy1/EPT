Additional uses for Evidence/Scheduling

 

Evidence and Scheduling are not simply restricted to Events.  This feature can also be set for Mechanisms although it may not always make sense to do so.  

 

Since Mechanisms can range from the observable to the very abstract, it may not be clear what detecting a particular Mechanism means.  For example, we may be able to detect a Mechanism such as “Defend Site A” through orders of a commander, but we may not be able to detect a Mechanism such as “Belief in Divine Justice” which may motivate an attack.

Tutorials

 

JCAT is designed as a flexible piece of software that allows you to work the way you want to.  The JCAT team has created a model based on the HBO television show The Sopranos to exhibit the versatility of the tool and to explore what it would take for Tony Soprano to commit the murder of his friend Salvatore Bonpensier.  

 

Within this model you will be able to exercise the use of various features such as timing, scheduling, evidence, delay, and persistence.  

 

As you will see there are other features in the tool which aren't mentioned at the moment which are utilized at a later time throughout the model building process.

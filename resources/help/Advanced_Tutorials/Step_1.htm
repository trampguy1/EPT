Creating a New Model

 

Step 1of 6:

 

The first step in starting any new model is to create a new model.  This may be accomplished by pressing the New Model Button  in the top toolbar or by selecting “File” from the Menu Bar and pressing “New.”  This will open a new blank Model which will appear in the Model Layout Frame.
